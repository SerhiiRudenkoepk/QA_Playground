Serhii Rudenko

QA Engineer & Creator of QA Background

Hi, I’m Serhii Rudenko! A QA Engineer with more than 5 years of hands-on experience in manual testing for AAA and mobile games (Ubisoft, Pingle Studio and others).

After years of working on big titles like Star Wars: Hunters, Watch Dogs: Legion and several NDA projects, I realized how difficult it can be for beginners to find practical, up-to-date resources that combine real theory with hands-on tools. That’s why I created QA Background.

This project is my way of giving back to the QA community - especially to those who are just starting their journey. Here what this site has:

  • ✅ Interactive quizzes based on the most frequently asked interview questions for Junior/Middle QA Software and GameDev positions (I will force our AI (and will, once again, search for the info myself) friend to find more questions for this too)
  • ✅ Practical testing tools - including my own Cats and Dogs REST API that you can download and use in Postman for real API testing practice
  • ❌ Theory + real case studies - WIP

My goal is to keep growing this platform: adding more hands-on tools, new practice APIs, challenges, and resources so that beginners and junior testers can train their skills in a safe, structured environment before facing real projects.

This site is both a learning platform for the QA community and my personal portfolio - a place where I show not only what I’ve done in the past, but what I’m actively building right now.

I'd love to hear your feedback and ideas! So feel free to check my LinkedIn and GitHub and drop me a message!
